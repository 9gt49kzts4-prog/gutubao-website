About Gutubao

Professional Airless Paint Sprayer Manufacturer

Founded in 2010, Nantong Gutubao Precision Machinery Co., Ltd. is a professional manufacturer specializing in the research, development, and production of airless paint spraying equipment.

Located in Haimen District, Nantong City, Jiangsu Province, China, in the heart of the Yangtze River Delta economic zone, Gutubao operates a modern manufacturing facility covering 18,000 square meters. The company employs more than 110 staff members, including over 30 engineers and technical specialists dedicated to product development and innovation.

Our factory is equipped with over 200 advanced manufacturing machines, including CNC machining centers and precision turning equipment. To ensure consistent product quality, we utilize professional inspection instruments such as ZEISS coordinate measuring machines, automatic gear inspection systems, spectrometers, projectors, roughness testers, coating thickness analyzers, and hardness testing equipment.

Gutubao integrates R&D, manufacturing, sales, and after-sales service under one roof.
